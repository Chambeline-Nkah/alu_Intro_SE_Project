Frequently Asked Questions

How can I enroll in the DigiHERoes courses?

Enrolling in our courses is simple. Just click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage, fill out the registration form, and you'll be ready to access the courses and start your digital learning journey.

Are the courses free to access?

Yes, all our courses are completely free. We believe in providing equal opportunities for all, especially for internally displaced girls. There are no hidden charges or fees.

What if I face technical issues while taking the courses?

If you encounter technical problems, don't worry. We have a dedicated support center to assist you. You can submit your queries by filling out this Support form and we will attend to your queries.

How can I earn achievement badges?

Earning achievement badges is a great way to track your progress. To earn badges, you need to complete specific milestones or demonstrate proficiency in each course. Once you've met the requirements, you'll automatically receive your badges.

Is there a specific schedule for the courses?

You have the flexibility to learn at your own pace. Our courses are available 24/7, so you can access the materials and lessons whenever it's convenient for you.

How do I know and choose the right course for me?
